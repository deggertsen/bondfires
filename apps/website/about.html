About Bondfires

We believe men deserve a better way to connect—one that respects their time, privacy, and need for authentic community.

Our Mission

Bondfires exists to help men build meaningful connections through thoughtful, asynchronous video conversations. We believe that men need authentic community—especially around life's biggest moments like crises, victories, fatherhood, and leadership—but traditional men's groups often don't fit into modern lives.

We've built a platform that eliminates scheduling conflicts, respects privacy, and enables deep conversations on your own time.

Our Values

Trust

We believe trust is built through transparency and security. That's why we use end-to-end encryption and don't harvest or sell your data. You own your conversations, and we protect them.

Ownership

Your data is yours. Your conversations are yours. Your time is yours. We built Bondfires to give you control over how and when you connect, not to extract value from your engagement.

Integrity

We're a non-profit focused on community, not profit. We're honest about our practices, clear about our intentions, and committed to building something that genuinely serves men's needs.

Why We Built Bondfires

The Scheduling Problem

Traditional men's groups require everyone to be online or in-person at the same time. For men with demanding careers, families, or other commitments, this creates "calendar tetris"—juggling schedules just to connect. We wanted a better way.

The Privacy Problem

Most digital platforms harvest and sell your data. Your conversations become products. We believe men's groups should be private, secure spaces—not data mines. That's why we built end-to-end encryption and a non-profit model.

The Depth Problem

Asynchronous conversations allow for more thoughtfulness. Instead of reacting in real-time, you can craft meaningful responses. This leads to deeper connections and more authentic exchanges than rapid-fire chat or surface-level check-ins.

The Accessibility Problem

Many men's groups require paid memberships or expensive retreats. We believe authentic community should be accessible. Our free core platform removes financial barriers while transparent premium options support sustainability.

The Founders

Bondfires was founded by two men in tech who experienced the challenges of finding authentic male community firsthand. With backgrounds in technology and a shared passion for helping men connect, we built Bondfires to solve the problems we faced.

We're building this because we believe men deserve better—better ways to connect, better privacy protection, and better community that fits into real life.
