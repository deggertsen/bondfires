How It Works

1. Spark a Bondfire

Record and share a video post about what's on your mind—crises, victories, fatherhood, leadership, or just life.

2. Respond with Video

Watch what others share and respond with your own video when you're ready. No scheduling, no calendar tetris.

3. Build Connections

Form meaningful relationships through thoughtful, asynchronous conversations on your own time.

What Makes Bondfires Different

Asynchronous Format

Connect on your own time. No calendar conflicts, no scheduling nightmares. Think, then respond when you're ready.

Privacy-First

End-to-end encryption. Non-profit model. No data harvesting. We respect your privacy because you own your conversations.

Intentional Structure

Camp rules, response limits, and guided prompts ensure depth over breadth. Quality conversations, not quantity.

Accessible Community

Free core platform with transparent premium options. No paid retreats required. Just real conversations.
