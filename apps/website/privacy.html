Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

At Bondfires, we believe privacy is fundamental to authentic community. We're committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and share your information when you use Bondfires.

Our Commitment to Privacy

We don't harvest your data. We don't sell your information. We don't use your conversations for advertising or profit. Bondfires is a non-profit focused on community, not data collection.

End-to-end encryption. Your video conversations are encrypted end-to-end, meaning only you and the participants in your bondfires can see what's shared. We can't read your conversations, even if we wanted to.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, username, and profile information you choose to share
  • Video Content: Videos you record and share in bondfires (encrypted end-to-end)
  • Profile Information: Display name, avatar, and any optional profile details you provide

Information We Collect Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version (for compatibility and support)
  • Usage Data: App features used, session duration, and general app interactions (for improving the service)
  • Technical Data: IP address, network information, and error logs (for security and troubleshooting)

What We Don't Collect

  • We don't track your location (unless you explicitly share it)
  • We don't access your contacts or address book
  • We don't read your text messages or other apps
  • We don't use cookies or tracking pixels for advertising

How We Use Your Information

We use your information only to:

  • Provide and improve the Bondfires service
  • Enable you to participate in bondfires and connect with other members
  • Respond to your support requests and inquiries
  • Ensure security and prevent abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

How We Protect Your Information

End-to-End Encryption

Your video conversations are encrypted end-to-end using industry-standard encryption protocols. This means:

  • Only you and the participants in your bondfires can decrypt and view your videos
  • We cannot read your conversations, even if requested by law enforcement
  • Your conversations are protected from interception or unauthorized access

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) for all data transfers
  • Encryption at rest for stored data
  • Secure authentication and access controls
  • Regular security audits and updates

Data Minimization

We collect only the information necessary to provide the service. We don't collect data "just in case" or for future purposes we haven't disclosed.

Information Sharing

We don't sell your data. We don't share your information with advertisers, data brokers, or third parties for marketing purposes.

Limited Sharing

We may share your information only in these limited circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly agree to share information
  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party service providers who help us operate the service (e.g., cloud hosting, payment processing) under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request (though encrypted content remains inaccessible)
  • Safety and Security: To protect the rights, property, or safety of Bondfires, our users, or others

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access Your Data: Request a copy of the information we hold about you
  • Delete Your Data: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Correct Your Data: Update or correct your profile information at any time
  • Export Your Data: Request an export of your videos and account information
  • Opt Out: Control what information you share and how it's used

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@bondfires.org.

Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide the service and fulfill the purposes described in this policy. When you delete your account, we delete or anonymize your data within 30 days, except where we're required to retain it for legal purposes.

Children's Privacy

Bondfires is intended for users aged 18 and older. We don't knowingly collect information from children under 18. If you're a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us immediately.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed and stored in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy, regardless of where it's processed.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

Email: privacy@bondfires.org
Website: bondfires.org

Your Consent

By using Bondfires, you consent to this Privacy Policy. If you don't agree with this policy, please don't use the service.
